1. Data Collection and Storage

We use Firebase Firestore and Firebase Storage services provided by Google to store your messages and other related data securely.

Messages

  • Messages sent through the app are stored in Firebase Firestore as structured data for efficient retrieval and synchronization across devices.
  • Attachments, such as images and videos, are stored in Firebase Storage. These files are linked to corresponding entries in Firestore for proper management.

2. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement the following measures to protect your information:

  • All data stored in Firebase Firestore and Storage is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard encryption protocols.

  • We periodically review and update our security practices to align with current standards and best practices.

The messages and attachments you send are used solely for the purpose of providing the core functionality of the app, such as:

  • Delivering messages to recipients in real-time.

We do not share or sell your data to third parties for marketing or other purposes.

4. User Control

You have full control over your data. You can delete messages and attachments at any time through the app. Once deleted, the data will be permanently removed from Firebase Firestore and Storage.
